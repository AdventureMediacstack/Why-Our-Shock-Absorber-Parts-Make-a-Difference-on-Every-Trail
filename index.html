Key Features and Benefits

We designed our rod ends to solve the real problems we’ve seen in the field. Here’s what sets ours apart:

1. Heavy-Duty Construction
Machined from high-strength steel, our rod ends handle extreme forces without bending, cracking, or wearing prematurely. They're built for high-impact environments like rock crawling, trail runs, and desert racing.

2. Spherical Bearing with High Misalignment Tolerance
Each rod end comes equipped with a spherical bearing that allows for smooth articulation at a variety of angles. This is key in long-travel applications or setups with dynamic geometry changes during suspension travel.

3. Corrosion Resistance
Whether you're in snow, mud, or dry desert heat, our protective coatings ensure your rod ends last through the elements and keep your shocks performing without compromise.

4. Easy Maintenance and Rebuildability
We know that off-road parts take a beating. That’s why these rod ends are designed to be easily inspected, replaced, or rebuilt without hassle giving you more time on the trail and less time on the jack stands.

5. Engineered Compatibility
Designed specifically for Offroad shocks, but also compatible with other high-performance shock bodies depending on your application. This makes them a versatile option for anyone upgrading or maintaining their suspension setup.
